About Us

Utkrishta Bharath

  India is home to over a billion people, accommodating incredible cultural diversity between languages, geographic regions, religious traditions and social stratifications. In recognition of this large demographic diversity, the following descriptions are not intended to represent every Indian person. However, there are common themes and principles that contribute to the values, attitudes, beliefs and norms of the dominant society. Generally speaking, Indians tend to have a strong sense of pride in the distinctiveness and diversity of their culture. For example, the country’s agricultural expansions and technological advancements in infrastructure, science and engineering are sources of pride. Moreover, a considerable amount of pride stems from India's rich artistic cultural exports of music, fine arts, literature and spirituality (especially the practice of yoga).

The Utkrishta Bharat club was founded in 2021-22 by a group of dedicated students and scholars who were passionate about promoting awareness of India's rich heritage and culture. They envisioned a club that would inspire and empower students to embrace the values of nation, culture, and civilization, and to cultivate their character and leadership skills. Through a variety of events and programs, the Utkrishta Bharat club seeks to foster a sense of pride and responsibility among students, and to encourage them to make a positive impact in their communities and in the world at large.


Since ancient times, Bharath has been known as the 'Sone Ki Chidiya' or the 'Golden Bird' due to its abundance of culture & tradition, rich heritage, and breathtaking art & architecture. Nations do not become great by accident. They become great because of their responsible citizens. Just as a building needs a robust foundation for support, the foundation of a strong nation lies in its youth who are empowered with cultural values, take pride in the country and are able and willing to contribute for its further development and growth.

National Institute of Technology Karnataka, is one of the premier institutes of India currently having NIRF rank 10 in the engineering category. The institute facilitates transformation of students into good human beings, responsible citizens and competent professionals, focusing on the assimilation, generation and dissemination of knowledge.

The name "Utkrishta Bharath" is composed of two Sanskrit words - "Utkrishta" and "Bharat". "Utkrishta" is an adjective that means elevated, exalted, or excellent. It is derived from the Sanskrit root "kṛṣhṭa" which means to lift up, raise, or exalt. "Bharath" is a Sanskrit term that has been used for India since ancient times. It is derived from the legendary king Bharata, who is said to have ruled over a vast empire that included much of the Indian subcontinent. So, the phrase "Utkrista Bharath" means an elevated or excellent India. It represents a vision of a prosperous and culturally rich India, and conveys a sense of pride and respect for the country's rich cultural heritage.

In this context, apart from promoting cultural awareness, the Utkrishta Bharath club also encourages students to actively participate in nation-building activities. The club recognizes that the true strength of a nation lies in its people, and seeks to empower students to make a positive impact on society. Through various projects and initiatives, the Utkrishta Bharath club encourages students to engage in social service activities that benefit the community. These activities range from organizing cleanliness drives, to providing education and healthcare services to underprivileged children. By promoting a culture of service and social responsibility, the Utkrishta Bharath club helps to shape responsible citizens who are committed to making a difference in the world.

One of the core values of the Utkrishta Bharath club is the importance of friendship and cooperation. The club recognizes that it takes a collective effort to bring about positive change, and seeks to foster a spirit of camaraderie among students. By providing a platform for students to connect and collaborate with each other, the Utkrishta Bharath club helps to build a strong sense of community on campus. This sense of community extends beyond the boundaries of the institute, as the club seeks to engage with the wider community through various outreach programs. By building bridges between the institute and the community, the Utkrishta Bharath club helps to promote social harmony and goodwill.

In summary, the Utkrishta Bharath club is a student organization that seeks to promote Indian culture and values among the students of the National Institute of Technology Karnataka.
